ABOUT

We are a diverse community of Bristol students and researchers who believe the potential for human flourishing is vast. Modern technologies pose existential risks (x-risk) to humanity. We aim to build an environment for work and study on x-risk and development of longtermist wisdom.

Key problems are posed by the threats of uncontrolled climate change, misaligned artificial intelligence (AI), nuclear warfare, and engineered pandemics. We invite action to reduce these specific risks as well as the project of building a wiser, resilient society for general risk reduction.

GET INVOLVED

Talks from academics and professionals are held throughout the academic year and are a great way to learn more about specific problems.

Interact with x-risk and those interested in it with our workshops. Learn to forecast and play out scenarios.

During the winter term you can participate in our x-risk introductory course: an eight week masterclass covering general x-risk philosophy and profiling specific problem areas.

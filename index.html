Building a diverse community of Bristol students and researchers who want to reduce existential risk and promote longtermist wisdom development. The potential for humanity's future flourishing is vast. We aim to create an environment for reasoning and researching the best possible futures for humanity. Modern technologies pose a serious existential risk to humanity. We aim to build a field of people dedicated to safeguarding humanity's future.
